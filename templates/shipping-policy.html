Shipping Policy

Fast and safe delivery across Egypt.

At Romaire Store, we are committed to delivering your orders safely and efficiently throughout Egypt. Below are the details of our shipping coverage, fees, and estimated delivery times.

1. Delivery Coverage

We deliver to all regions across Egypt.

2. Shipping Fees

Standard Delivery Fees:

Cairo & Giza: 75 EGP
Delta & Canal Cities: 100 EGP
Saina & Sharm El Saikh & Marsa Alam & Al Wadi El-gedid: 180 EGP
Down Egypt: 120 EGP
Marsa Matruh & North Coast & Gardaka : 130 EGP

3. Delivery Timeframes

We work with trusted shipping partners to ensure prompt delivery. Estimated processing and transit times are:

  • Cairo & Giza: 2-3 business days
  • Delta & Canal Cities: 3-4 business days
  • Down Egypt: 4-5 business days
  • Remote Areas: 4-5 business days

Note: Delivery times may be affected by unforeseen delays, holidays, or logistical circumstances.
